The aim of this site is to randomdly generate questions for anime fans to test the depth of their knowledge on characters and shows.

The languages and frameworks used are html, css, javascript and react.

I used RestApi's to get the questions from a database. It was a pretty challenging project because it forced me to be certain that I understood the basics of react such as components, props, useEffects and useStates.

It really left me excited, thinking of other fun projects I could work on in the future.
